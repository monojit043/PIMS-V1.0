About PIMS

v1.0 · Engineers India Limited

PIMS (Piping Isometrics Management System) is a comprehensive digital solution for managing, reviewing, and approving pipeline isometric drawings across EPC projects at Engineers India Limited.

It enables seamless collaboration between Modellers, Process Checkers, Material Checkers, Stress Checkers, and General Leaders — ensuring efficient workflow throughout the isometric lifecycle.

Centralised document management & version control
Real-time notifications & task tracking
Multi-role approval workflow
Stress-critical line identification
Comprehensive audit trails & reporting
